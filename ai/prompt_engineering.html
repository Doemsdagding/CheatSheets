Clean prompt
a prompt should be clean, consise, correctly spelled, and have corect use of punctions. Dont add uncessary information as it will make the ai lose focus.

Knowledge-level contraints
Sometimes telling the ai what you are (or to roplay as something) ensure the ai's output is relevant,consumable and focussed by controlling the ai's jargon and level of depth.

command

Goal
Explain like i'm 10
 Maximum simplicity priotirze what and why over how.
Explain like i know nothing about...
 Prevents to use of jargon, focus on fundamentals and gives necessary defintions.

Example
Explain what a neural network is, how it "learns," and what it's used for in simple, everyday terms. Tell it to me like I am a 10-year-old who loves building with LEGOs. Keep the explanation under three short paragraphs.

Use bulletpoints
Using bulletpoints results in a sort of checklist for the ai ensuring it adresses every specific requirements and constraint

Example
Act as an Expert Python Developer. Your task is to write a Python script that takes a CSV file, cleans the data, and outputs a refined DataFrame.

Input File Details:
  •     File Name: raw_sales_data.csv
  •     Columns: TransactionID, Customer_Age, Sale_Amount
Required Cleaning Steps:
  •     Handling Missing Data: Remove any rows where the Sale_Amount is missing (NaN).
  •     Data Transformation: Convert the Sale_Amount column from a string (currency format) to a float.
  •     Outlier Treatment: Filter out all records where the Customer_Age is less than 18 or greater than 95.
  •     Output: Print the total number of rows removed during the cleaning process and display the first 5 rows of the final cleaned DataFrame.

Ai Roleplaying
Making an AI model roleplay a specific role is a good idea in prompt engineering primarily because it establishes a clear context, persona, and set of constraints for the model's output. This dramatically improves the relevance, consistency, and quality of the generated response.Roleplaying as an "expert"  often encourages the model to generate more detailed, authoritative, and structured responses, drawing deeper on its training data related to that domain.
